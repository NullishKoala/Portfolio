about yuuyuu

our story

Why did we choose yuuyuu as the name of our restaurant? Because in Japanese, ゆうゆう yuuyuu means 'quiet, calm, leisurely,' while ゆうゆうじてき 'yuuyuujiteki' means 'living quietly and comfortably free from worldly cares'. We want our guests to feel that way when they visit yuuyuu.

bookings

At yuuyuu we have an easy but strict rule - we book only single tables. Every table is located in a separate booth, so our guest can enjoy a meal in peace. We strive to make yuuyuu an inclusive place. Please let us know if you require adjustments to the light, noise, or temperature at your booth.

device policy

We want our guests to focus on enjoying their meals without any distractions, so there is another important rule to follow when you dine at yuuyuu - no electronic devices at the table. We kindly ask to leave all electronic devices on mute in the cloakroom on a dedicated shelf.

menu

Our menu features a plant-based fusion kitchen.

All yuuyuu restaurants were awarded Michelin stars last year!

Our chefs are here to cater to your needs; please notify us about your dietary restrictions. The menu changes according to the season, and we use products only from local farmers to shorten the delivery time from the field to the table as much as possible. The yuuyuuportion sizes are adjusted for individual dining, making it easier to combine and enjoy a few selections from the menu. All items on the menu complement each other.
